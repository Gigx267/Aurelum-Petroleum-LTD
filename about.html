Energy • Resources • Sustainability

AURELUM GROUP operates at the intersection of energy, logistics, and resource management, enabling efficient value chains that support sustainable development and international industry needs.

With headquarters in Accra and operational hubs in Tema and Düsseldorf, we align Africa's strategic resource potential with global demand—delivering reliable supply, optimized logistics, and ESG-aligned solutions across continents.

Our diversified approach spans petroleum products, solar energy, precious minerals, and integrated supply chain management, all built on strict compliance, transparent processes, and sustainable growth principles.

  • Strategic sourcing and commodity trading across energy and mineral sectors
  • Integrated logistics and export coordination for efficient global delivery
  • ESG-aligned governance embedded across all operations
  • Renewable energy solutions accelerating Africa's green transition

We partner with governments, producers, and industrial buyers to create resilient, compliant, and commercially competitive value chains that drive long-term impact across Africa and beyond.
