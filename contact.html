Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

Our Ghana offices operate from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM GMT, Monday to Friday. Our Germany office operates from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM CET, Monday to Friday. Emergency contacts are available 24/7 for urgent matters.

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please use our emergency contact numbers for immediate assistance.

Do you offer customized solutions?

Yes, we specialize in creating tailored solutions for our clients' specific needs. Our team will work closely with you to understand your requirements and develop a customized approach that delivers optimal results.

What regions do you serve?

We operate primarily across Africa and Europe, with our main hubs in Ghana and Germany. However, we have the capability to serve clients globally through our extensive network of partners and logistics providers.
