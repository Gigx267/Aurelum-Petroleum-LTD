About Us

At AURELUM P LTD with bases in Accra, Tema, and Düsseldorf, our company aligns African resource potential with global demand. We operate with strict adherence to compliance, transparency, and ESG-aligned principles. We specialize in the international trade and logistics of petroleum products, LPG, Naphtha, and precious minerals. Our mission is to bridge the energy and mining sectors through transparent, efficient, and sustainable supply chains across Africa, Europe, and global markets.

Our Services

Petroleum Trading

We supply premium-grade crude, refined products, diesel, gasoline and specialty petroleum materials to strategic markets across Africa, the Middle East and Europe.

  • Certified quality assurance
  • Competitive pricing models
  • Secure logistics and delivery
  • Global market access

LPG & Naphtha Supply

AURELUM facilitates sourcing and timely delivery of LPG and Naphtha for industrial, commercial and governmental clients through strong supplier relations and stable availability.

  • Reliable supply chains
  • Strategic supplier partnerships
  • Timely delivery assurance
  • Competitive market pricing

Precious Minerals Trading

Through vetted partnerships we trade gold, manganese and high‑value minerals originating from West Africa. All exports adhere to due‑diligence, traceability and international regulatory standards.

  • Vetted mining partnerships
  • Full traceability and compliance
  • International regulatory adherence
  • Quality certification

Logistics & Export Coordination

End‑to‑end logistics support including port operations, documentation, customs clearance, warehousing and secure shipment arrangements to optimize cost and shorten lead times.

  • Comprehensive documentation
  • Customs clearance expertise
  • Optimized shipping routes
  • Cost-efficient solutions
